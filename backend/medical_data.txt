Disease: Malaria
Symptoms: Fever, chills, headache, nausea, muscle pain.
Transmission: Mosquito bites (Anopheles).
Treatment: Antimalarial drugs like Chloroquine or ACT.
Prevention: Mosquito nets, repellents, stagnant water removal.

Disease: Typhoid Fever
Symptoms: High fever, weakness, stomach pain, headache, loss of appetite.
Transmission: Contaminated food or water (Salmonella Typhi).
Treatment: Antibiotics (Ciprofloxacin, Azithromycin).
Prevention: Hand washing, safe water, vaccination.

Disease: Common Cold
Symptoms: Runny nose, sneezing, sore throat, cough, mild fever.
Transmission: Viral infection (rhinovirus) via air or touch.
Treatment: Rest, fluids, OTC decongestants.
Prevention: Hand hygiene, avoiding close contact.

Disease: Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)
Symptoms: Often none ("silent killer"), but can include headaches or shortness of breath.
Causes: High salt intake, lack of exercise, genetics.
Treatment: ACE inhibitors, beta-blockers, lifestyle changes (dash diet).
Prevention: Regular exercise, low sodium diet, stress management.

Disease: Diabetes (Type 2)
Symptoms: Increased thirst, frequent urination, fatigue, blurred vision.
Causes: Insulin resistance, sedentary lifestyle, obesity.
Treatment: Metformin, insulin therapy, blood sugar monitoring.
Prevention: Healthy eating, physical activity, weight loss.

Disease: Dengue Fever
Symptoms: High fever, severe headache, pain behind eyes, joint/muscle pain, rash.
Transmission: Aedes mosquito bites.
Treatment: Fluids, pain relief (avoid Ibuprofen/Aspirin due to bleeding risk).
Prevention: Eliminating mosquito breeding sites.
